About me

I am Khayal Abbas Akhtar , Currently working as Visiting Faculty at Iqra University , Islamabad .I am a Machine Learning enthusiastic researcher with background in Software Engineerng and passion for Computer Science.

I became interested in Machine Learning in 2018, as I discovered that many abstract mathematical objects that I learned during college have very interesting applications to real-world problems.

I have been involved with different area related development projects at various level as well as having education with the focus of Artificial Intelligence & Higher Programming Languages in multicultural international environment from a European University. I always been fascinated by the R&D side of computer science and software.

In 2018 , Government of Slovakia awarded me with scholarship to study at University of Zilina, Slovakia among the 19 people from all over the world. I have development experience in Artificial Intelligence and Software development from RISE research Center NUST, and Internet of Things(IoT) as well as embedded system from my final year dissertation .

Interests

My main interest is Deep Learning and its applications to generative models (in particular variational autoencoders), reinforcement learning and information geometry.

My goal is to use my mathematical background to ellaborate a more theoretical view towards deep learning and to better understand the true reasons behind the success of neural networks.

I also like to implement and test my ideas! I have experience with Python and Deep Learning libraries, such as Tensorflow and Torch.

I do believe that there is nothing better than working on what one enjoys, as a member of a team of great people.
